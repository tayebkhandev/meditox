female Hair Transplant

Hair loss is a primary concern for many women. The hair loss in women can start at any age due to variant reason; such as stress, gene’s and hormones. Unlike men, female become self-conscious about their hair loss condition and starts searching for a way to resolve it. Unfortunately, the female hair loss pattern is way more complex than men and if a woman notices the hair loss in the early stage; it’s impossible for her to undermine that the hair loss is temporary or permanent. The revival of hair growth treatment for women is few, which are not very effective. All around the world; only 5% of women become a good candidate for the hair transplant procedure. Mostly women suffer from diffuse hair loss that involves thinning in all areas of the scalp which resulted in having no donor area in the end. After getting rejected from the hair transplant procedure; women are left with only one option to go for the drugs that treat hair loss; which is a time taken process, generate very slow result and contain some side effects as well. For female hair loss, the only FDA’s approved drug is minoxidil, which you need to directly apply on the scalp. However, minoxidil is not a miracle treatment, it will give some hair back, but not restore all the hair density that has lost. This drug is not going to produce the fast result; you have to wait 3 months or more to see that the drug works or not. If the drug work, then you have to keep using it to maintain the hair growth; if you stop using these drugs after a certain time period their is a probability that you will again get acquainted with the hair loss. Another drug Propecia (androgen blocker) is used to counter the female hair loss; if the minoxidil is not working. Although FDA has not approved this drug to treat the female hair loss; due to the fact that it deals with the hormones and can cause many serious medical problems; explained by Rana Irfan, hair transplant surgeon, who commonly treats female hair loss issues in Islamabad.

What is P.R.P Treatment

History explains that P.R.P has been widely used since 1970’s in orthopedics, dentistry, plastic surgery and now the hair restoration field has adopted this method. For decades, It’s been a wish of the physician and patient opting for the hair loss procedure; is to make the procedure tolerable and less painful, least time consuming which generate satisfying result in less time. In this context; P.R.P treatment to deal female hair loss issues in Islamabad has taken a prominent place. P.R.P is a platelet rich plasma therapy in which the blood platelets are used to stimulate the hair growth. Human blood cells contain growth factor that basically is-proteins. These proteins contain regenerating properties that work to repair the damages tissue and also activate the stem cells. P.R.P, is a very simple procedure in which blood is drawn out of the patient’s body; then plasma (a yellow substance) is obtained from the blood and then the extracted plasma is applied to the scalp to make the dormant hair follicle active and to stimulate the hair growth. This procedure of P.R.P in Islamabad does not require long sitting it only take a few hours from your busy schedule.
