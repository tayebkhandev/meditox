Eyebrows Transplant Procedure

Eyelash transplant is a safe procedure. The eyelash hair transplant is done in Pakistan with both methods; FUE or FUT. The hair transplant surgeon would recommend the best method for you; after analyzing the patient’s demands and requirements. The procedure is similar to the scalp hair transplant in which hairs are taken out from the donor area and placed in the required area. The eyelash hair transplant is normally performed under oral sedative and the required time for this procedure can be maximum two hours. Typically in one session 25 to 50 hairs can be transplanted in each eyelid. The main thing a patient need to understand before having the surgery that after eyelash transplant the eyelashes curled has to be done manually in order to retrieve the sloping curve of natural eyelash hairs.
