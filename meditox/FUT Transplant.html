FUT_Transplantation

Hair Transplantation is a procedure in which we take hair follicles (follicular unit/grafts) from one area of the scalp, called “donor area”, and transplant them to another area of the scalp, called “recipient area”. There are two major techniques that are used for this procedure, one is known as FUE (Follicular Unit Excision) and another one is FUT (Follicular Unit Transplant). Both techniques are equally beneficial for treating both male and female hair loss patterns. Since the major difference in FUT and FUE is only about harvesting the grafts and rest of the procedure is same in both techniques, therefore FUT is considered to be good in Eyebrows and Eyelashes transplant, as well as hair transplant on accidental and burn scars. As scalp hair normally found in the form of a group of hair, like 1-4 hair in one graft, so we place them in a follicular unit. This is the main reason the technique is known as FUT (Follicular Unit Transplant). Dr. Rana Irfan is proficient and has been performing FUT for over 2 decades. He has developed this technique to a new level of safety and quality. Follicular Unit Transplant (FUT) is the traditional hair transplant method which involves extracting a linear strip of hair-bearing skin from the back or the side of the scalp. The strip is then dissected to separate individual grafts
