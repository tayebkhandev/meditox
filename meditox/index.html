Who is Dr Rana Irfan

Dr. Rana Irfan is an American Board Certified Hair Restoration surgeon (ABHRS). He is a Fellow of the International Society of Hair Restoration Surgery (FISHRS). He is the only Asian doctor working as an oral protocol examiner in ABHRS USA for the last six years. He is also working as a member of the credential committee and member of examination committee ABHRS. He was elected to be the member of the board of directors of ABHRS.
He is the author of many publications of world level forums related to hair restoration surgery. He has performed more than 20,000 successful hair restoration procedures since he is professional with extensive experience and he used the latest tools of the moment. He has been training doctors on hair transplant surgery for the last 20 years.
