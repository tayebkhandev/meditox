Hair transplant helps to bring back the full head of hair. If thinning up the top and going bald really bothers you, a hair transplant can be one way to get back lost hair and feel more confident about your looks. In hair transplant surgery, the hair that you already have will move to the area where you don't have hair. Hair transplantation has been started since the 1950s, but procedures have changed a lot in recent years. The most common procedures are FUE (Follicular Unit Extraction) and FUT (Follicular Unit Transplantation). The type of technique used depends on your hair type, age, quality and the size of the area where you’re getting the hair transplant.
