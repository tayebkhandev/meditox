IFI Hair Transplant

Irfan Follicular Implant (IFI) is the hair transplant procedure designed by Dr. Rana Irfan. The goal of this hair transplant technique is to minimize the wastage of grafts and to save the Donor Hair grafts for the further hair transplant surgeries (if required). Irfan follicular implant (IFI) is very similar to the Follicular Unit Transplantation (FUT); in which the strip of skin bearing hair is extracted from the back of the head. In order to extract the strip; a surgeon gives 2 blind parallel cuts of about 20-25cm long and of width 1.0 to 2.00cms in the donor area. Surgeon Rana Irfan pointed out the fact that the hair at the back of the head are at 25 (degree) drop angle. According to surgeon Rana Irfan estimation in each blind cut that is given in Follicular unit transplantation (FUT) approximately 700/800 HAIRS get wasted, which in total cause lost of 1600 grafts in the donor area. Basically the Irfan follicular implant (IFI) is designed to reduce the grafts wastage of FUT. In Irfan follicular Implant (IFI) the strip is harvested by using an instrument that separates the skin- save the donor area from the parallel blind cuts. From the skin separator usage; no grafts get wasted, even it gives the liability to obtain more grafts without wasting a single hair. Basically the stretch in the donor closure is the ultimate cause of scarring in Follicular unit Transplantation (FUT). The separator instrument in the donor area pushes the skin from both ends, which increases the skin laxity that resulted in causing less stretch in the donor closure. Also, the technique of Irfan follicular implant (IFI) has comparatively caused less pain than Follicular unit transplantation (FUT). The main benefit this Irfan follicular implant (IFI) that it reduces the stretch in the donor closure which cause minimal scarring and also subside pain. Other benefits include that the patientâ€™s acquire more density through Irfan follicular Implant (IFI) because the quantity of grafts extracted in Irfan follicular Implant (IFI) is higher than the grafts acquire in FUT hair transplant.
