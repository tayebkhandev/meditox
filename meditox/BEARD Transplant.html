Beard Hair Transplant

Beard hair transplant is a surgical procedure to transplant hair in facial region which lacks the density and fullness. Usually; it is done to improve the hair density. Facial surgery has highly been practiced by men; who get acquainted with the poor hair growth, patches or any facial scars that cause them hair loss. Beard hair transplant is the most popular method; performed at the goatee, mustache, beard, and at cheeks to increase the amount of hairs persist in that region.
